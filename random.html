Random Fun Facts

  • 1 in 4 websites are blocked in China that are normally accessible through search engines like google
  • 1.72 billion people are affected by internet censorship. Over 700 million of those people are in China
  • 80% of the world does not have access to uncensored internet
  • China employs more than 30000 agents to investigate internet users
  • Everything you do online is recorded in China
  • If the government finds anything you put online offensive, you can get a fine up to $1800 in China.
